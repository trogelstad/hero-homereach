Featured Tool

Start with the Colorado Assistance Explorer.

The Colorado Assistance Explorer is being built as a cleaner, scalable, interactive way to explore statewide, regional, rural, mountain, local, and hero-focused homebuyer assistance paths.

It is not designed to tell buyers they qualify for a program. It is designed to help them understand possible paths, learn what questions to ask, and prepare for conversations with a qualified lender, housing counselor, real estate professional, or program administrator.

Important Note

Use these tools as educational starting points.

Hero HomeReach tools are designed to help buyers understand the landscape, prepare better questions, and avoid common confusion. Program availability and eligibility vary. These tools do not provide an eligibility determination and do not replace guidance from a qualified lender, housing counselor, real estate professional, or program administrator.
