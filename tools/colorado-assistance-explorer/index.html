Assistance Pathways

Explore by strategy type.

Many buyers need more than one answer. These pathways help organize the major categories worth discussing.

CHFA Statewide Programs

metroDPA

VA + Assistance Strategy

Eligible military buyers may want to ask how VA benefits, seller credits, and certain assistance paths may interact.
