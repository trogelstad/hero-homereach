Common Questions

Before You Book

A few things people ask before scheduling their first call.

Is the consultation really free?
Yes — completely. There is no cost, no catch, and no obligation to do anything after the call. Hero HomeReach™ is a guidance service, not a lender.
Will I be pressured to buy or apply for anything?
Never. Our job is to educate you on what you qualify for. What you do with that information is entirely up to you. No pressure, ever.
What should I have ready for the call?
Just yourself and a rough sense of your situation — your profession, income range, and whether you've bought a home before. We'll do the rest.
I have student loans. Do I still qualify?
Often yes. Many Colorado programs account for student debt and have qualifying structures that work for educators and healthcare workers with loans.
I already have a VA loan — is there anything else?
Yes. Colorado state and local programs can often be stacked on top of VA benefits, potentially adding $10,000–$25,000 in additional assistance.
How long does the whole process take?
The call is 30 minutes. Finding and applying for programs varies, but most heroes have a clear picture within a week of their consultation.
