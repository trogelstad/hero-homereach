Overview

Hero HomeReach™ ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at herohomereach.com or interact with our services.

By using our website or submitting your information, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and any other information you voluntarily provide through our contact forms, eligibility quiz, or consultation booking system.
  • Usage Data: Information your browser automatically sends, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site.
  • Quiz Responses: Answers you provide through our eligibility quiz, including profession, estimated credit score, income range, and homebuying timeline.
  • Communication Data: Any messages or information you send us directly.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized homeownership education and guidance
  • Generate your eligibility quiz results
  • Schedule and facilitate free consultations
  • Send relevant educational information and updates (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party tools we use to operate our business, including CRM platforms (HubSpot), email marketing services (MailerLite), and scheduling tools (Calendly). These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of others.
  • With Your Consent: If you explicitly authorize us to share your information with a specific third party, such as a CHFA-approved lender.

We will never share your information with advertisers or unaffiliated marketing companies without your explicit consent.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of our website.

Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.
