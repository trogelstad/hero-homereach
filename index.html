Available Programs

Three Types of Assistance. All Free to Explore.

Colorado offers multiple program types that can often be combined. Here's what heroes typically access.

🏦
Up to $25,000
in down payment assistance

Down Payment Assistance

DPA programs reduce or eliminate the barrier of saving a down payment. Available as second mortgages with low or deferred payments.

  • CHFA programs available statewide
  • Income limits up to $150K in many areas
  • Works with FHA, conventional, VA loans
  • Available to repeat buyers in many programs
🎁
Up to $10,000
in outright grants

Grants

True grants don't need to be repaid. Colorado and local municipalities offer grant funding specifically earmarked for public service heroes.

  • No repayment required
  • Employer-assisted housing grants available
  • Local municipality programs by county
  • Can often stack with DPA programs
✂️
Up to $15,000
in forgivable loans

Forgivable Loans

Forgivable loans are structured to be forgiven, typically over 3–5 years as long as you remain in the home. Essentially free money for committed buyers.

  • Forgiven after qualifying period
  • No monthly payments while in home
  • Teacher Next Door program
  • Local government forgivable programs
